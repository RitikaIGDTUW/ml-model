Laws and Resources for Different Types of Abuse

Sexual abuse laws safeguard against sexual assault, harassment, and exploitation. Key legal provisions include:

  1. Indian Penal Code (IPC), 1860

    • Section 354: Outlaws assault or criminal force used to outrage a womanâ€™s modesty.
    • Section 354A: Defines and punishes sexual harassment, including unwelcome physical contact, advances, and sexually colored remarks.
    • Section 354B: Punishes assault or use of criminal force to disrobe a woman.
    • Section 375: Defines rape and its various forms, including sexual intercourse without consent.
    • Section 376: Provides punishment for rape, with stringent penalties including life imprisonment.
    • Section 376C: Addresses sexual abuse by people in authority (e.g., public servants, managers).
    • Section 376D: Punishes gang rape, with more severe penalties.
    • Section 509: Punishes acts intended to insult the modesty of a woman, including verbal or physical harassment of a sexual nature.

  2. The Protection of Children from Sexual Offences(POCSO) Act, 2012

    3. Protects minors from sexual abuse, including sexual assault, harassment, and pornography, with specific provisions for aggravated cases.

  3. Criminal Law (Amendment) Act, 2013 (Nirbhaya Act)

    • Strengthened the laws for sexual violence, making punishment for rape, gang rape, and other forms of sexual assault more stringent.
    • Introduced Section 354C (voyeurism) and Section 354D (stalking), which cover forms of sexual abuse related to observation and following with sexual intent.

  4. The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition, and Redressal) Act, 2013

    5. Specifically addresses sexual harassment at the workplace, offering protection and remedies for women facing abuse in professional settings.

  5. The Immoral Traffic (Prevention) Act, 1956

    • Deals with sexual abuse in the context of trafficking, particularly addressing exploitation and abuse related to forced prostitution.
    • These laws cover various forms of sexual abuse, providing women with legal recourse for protection and justice.
